## Core Philosophy & Values

### "More than coding"

> "Wspolczesny swiat nowych technologii to cos wiecej niz kodowanie. Na styku biznesu i programowania najlepiej sprawdzaja sie osoby, ktore potrafia myslec kreatywnie, rozwiazywac problemy, pracowac w zespole i komunikowac sie z innymi."

("The modern world of new technology is more than just coding. At the intersection of business and programming, people who can think creatively, solve problems, work in teams, and communicate with others thrive best.")

### Brand Values (distilled)

1. **Holistic developer growth** — Technical skills alone are not enough. Developers need creative thinking, problem-solving, teamwork, and communication skills.
2. **Well-rounded specialists** — The goal is to create not just great programmers, but conscious, versatile professionals.
3. **Market-relevant competencies** — Focus on skills that are actually demanded by the industry and useful in daily work.
4. **Accessible, diverse learning formats** — Knowledge delivered through articles, podcasts, newsletters, courses, workshops, and free ebooks.
5. **Staying current** — Helping developers keep up with trends and find new directions for professional growth.
6. **Community over isolation** — Building a supportive environment where programmers learn and grow together.
7. **Quality & practical focus** — Courses built around practical projects, individual code reviews, and mentoring.

### Mission Statement

> "Przeprogramowani to miejsce, w ktorym ambitni programisci znajda cos dla siebie. Przekazujemy wiedze o programowaniu poprzez artykuly, podcasty, blogi i newslettery, abys byl zawsze na biezaco i zawsze mogl znalezc nowy kierunek wlasnego rozwoju."

("Przeprogramowani is a place where ambitious programmers will find something for themselves. We share programming knowledge through articles, podcasts, blogs, and newsletters so you can stay up-to-date and always find new directions for your development.")

### Goal Statement

> "Naszym celem jest tworzenie srodowiska, w ktorym kazdy moze rozwijac kompetencje potrzebne na rynku pracy. Chcemy, zebys byl nie tylko swietnym programista, ale rowniez swiadomym i wszechstronnym specjalista. Tworzymy tresci, ktore pomoga ci stac sie lepszym w tym, co robisz."

("Our goal is to create an environment where everyone can develop the skills needed in the job market. We want you to be not just a great programmer, but also a conscious and versatile specialist. We create content that will help you become better at what you do.")

Przeprogramowani positions itself as:

1. **Educational authority** — Professional courses taught by senior engineers with real industry experience

3. **Holistic education** — Beyond coding: soft skills, business acumen, and career development
4. **Multi-format content leader** — Courses, articles, podcast, newsletter, YouTube, workshops
5. **Cutting-edge technology adopters** — Early movers on AI, latest frameworks and tooling
6. **Polish tech community pillar** — Exclusively Polish-language, serving the Polish developer ecosystem
